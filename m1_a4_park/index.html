Polnareff Land's Famous Attractions

Here are but a few of the great experiences you can experience only at Polnareff Land:

Iced Teacup Splashers

When traveling the desert for days, Polnareff drank iced tea with the Stardust Crusaders to be refreshed. Sit in tea cups that you control and dodge the teapot's iced tea from falling onto you!

Ferris Wheel of Nightmares

Parks are not complete without a ferris wheel, but they lack originality. Sponsored by Mannish Boy, Venture into a ferris wheel cart if you dare. You will face something from you or your fellow guests' nightmares.

Coco Jumbo's Presidential Theater

Get a test of the Mr. President room inside a giant Coco Jumbo replica! Families can have fun inside the giant room or visit the Presidential Theater to watch a movie.

Little Roma

Rome left an impression on Polnareff in more ways than one. Come and visit a small rome to get a taste of Italy! Youc an even meet Giorno, Mista, Trish, and even Rohan and Koichi in the Passione museum.

Jean Pierre Polnareff

The honorable, silver-haired frenchman, Jean Pierre Polnareff, always had a dream to open the best theme park in the world one day. After an incredible life-long journey, Polnareff was finally able to make his dream come true. He hopes you can have fun and experience the events he experienced in this one of a kind park!
